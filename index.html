A Complete Rethink Of Boys Education

Boy Brain brings three (3) main changes to boy’s education.

      1. A Career Focused education.

      2. An education that Includes Video Game play in boy’s learning.

      3. An education that is more Interactive Video based.

The Best Education A Boy Can Get

Career Focused

Boys will have extensive professional training in finding and preparing for a career,

Game Oriented

Some of the lessons will have a game version of them, were a lesson will be give and instead of a normal quiz, they play a game and as there character moves around the game, they have to answer quiz questions to move ahead in the game, boys can compete against them selves or form small teams and compete against each other,

Video Tutorials

A Typical Boy Brain School Day

Get to school early, it’s open at 7 a.m. now because boys in the Boy Brain program mostly work independently, go to home room, get logged in to computer. Check current marks and ranking against self, class, school, city, province/state, and country.

Start watching 20-minute math lesson, then take ten question practice quiz in the form of an action video game, don’t finish in the 15-minute time frame and lose video game, rewatch 20-minute math lesson, retake quiz again and win, score gets added automatically to math grade, game gives him scores of the other boys in his class, but not their names. Watches next math lesson.

8 a.m., teacher comes into class, she takes attendance and asked if anyone needs help, tells class to carry on with online training. She can go online and check all the students progress and see if any one is falling behind, no one is, she goes across the hall to help another teacher with her class.

At lunch time some of the boys retake the math quiz video game, to see if they can get an even higher score, if they do, the computer will replace their score with the new high score on their math grade.

Because most of the Boy Brain learning is online and video based, boys can rewatch lessons until they understand the material, and retake quiz's to help them test their understanding of the course material.
